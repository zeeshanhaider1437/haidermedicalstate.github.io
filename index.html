Haider medical state includes an institute and a medical college alongside an AI research centre. It contains modern facilities for learning medicine and treatment of patients. The institute trains students in various medical fields after matriculation. It has highly qualified staff accompanied with modern theatres and labs for practical learning. The college offers various courses: FSC, dispenser, lab technician, OT technician, ECG and X-ray technician. The hospital has various departments like emergency, outdoor, indoor wards, and operation theatres. Consultation by different practitioners is provided daily. AI education facilities are provided to students through a computer lab.

Medical field has produced a number of miracles in the last two centuries around the globe. In Pakistan, it is one of the leading professions. In contemporary times, the need for medical professionals is increasing due to the rise in diseases in the 21st century. Modern equipment supports diagnosis, yet it remains a challenging field. Adequate learning is essential to help people in need. Best learning depends on course selection and the mentor’s ability to deliver. Students are evaluated through examinations, and passing all stages prepares them to lead juniors in the future.
